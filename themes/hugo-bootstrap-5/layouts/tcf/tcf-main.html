Welcome to TCF 2025!

We are thrilled to invite you to the 2025 UBC Technical Career Fair (TCF)!

UBC stands among Canada's top in Engineering & Technology and is recognized globally as 1st for industry impact, innovation and infrastructure. We're proud of our commitment to technical excellence and inclusivity across Computer Science, Engineering, Arts and Business. In collaboration with women-focused tech groups, the LGBTQ+ community and hackathon initiatives, this year’s TCF stands as a nexus for technical prowess and empowerment. Engage with a community of 3,000 students at UBC's premier technical career fair. Don’t miss this opportunity—secure your spot now!
