Event Day Logistics

< back to main page

Last updated: January 22, 2026

This page hosts the logistics for the day of the event for employers. If you have any issues, on the day please contact your nearest TCF Volunteer or message Muhammad at the phone number listed in the TCF 2026 Day-of Details email.

Location Details

The TCF will take place at the AMS Great Hall in the AMS Student Nest located at 6133 University Blvd, Vancouver, BC V6T 1Z1.

Parking Details

There are several nearby parking lots. The closest include:

  • University Blvd Lot — next door to venue: 6131 University Boulevard, Vancouver, BC V6T 0C7, $4.50/hour; no maximum
  • Health Sciences Parkade — 6 minute walk to venue: 2250 Health Sciences Mall, Vancouver, BC V6T 1Z3, $2.25/half hour; $22 maximum
  • North Parkade — 5 minute walk to venue: 6115 Student Union Boulevard, Vancouver, BC V6T 1Z1, $2.25/half hour; $22 maximum

We are subject to the university's public parking so space may be limited. We are unable to provide vouchers for parking.

There is also metered parking available on the street, but we do not recommend this as it is very limited and expensive.

For an interactive map with parking rates, please visit the following and search for the AMS Student Nest: https://parking.ubc.ca/map.

Material Drop Off Details

The closest drop-off area to the AMS Great Hall is by the University Blvd Parking lot or anywhere in the cul-de-sac area closest to Brown's Crafthouse and the AMS Nest. If you prefer to drop off materials before parking, we suggest dropping them off here and contacting us beforehand. Please check your email for our phone details. We will have a team of volunteers near all doors between 8:50 AM and 10:00 AM.

Building Details

The Great Hall is located on the second floor of the AMS Nest. We will have signage on the day along with volunteers to direct you to the correct location.

We recommend using the elevator by the Wireless Wave for the fastest movement of materials.

Venue Details

  • The Great Hall is a large open space for booths.
  • There are no walls or dividers between booths, so please be respectful of your neighbours.
  • We have been told that the estimated amperage is 12A and the estimated voltage is 120V.
  • Wifi should be accessible through UBC Public Wifi. See the Internet Access section below for instructions.
  • There are no coat racks so please keep this in mind when packing.

Please see the floor plan below. There may be slight adjustments up to the date, please check the website for the latest updates.
