Event Day Logistics

< back to main page

This page hosts the logistics for the day of the event for employers. If you have any issues, on the day please contact your nearest TCF Volunteer or message Iris at the phone number listed in the employer email.
The event will take place at the Jack Poole Hall in the Robert H. Lee Alumni Centre on Wednesday January 22, 2025 from 9am-5pm.
It is open to the public from 10am-4pm. Set-up is from 9am-10am, and tear-down is from 4pm-5pm.
Use the drop down on the right side of the screen to navigate to the schedule below.

Location Details

We will be hosting the event in the Jack Poole Hall of the Robert H. Lee Alumni Centre at UBC.

Parking Details

There are multiple parking lots for employers to park in. The closest parking areas appear to be University Blvd Parkade and the North Parkade. We are subject to the university's public parking so space may be limited. We are unable to provide vouchers for parking.

There is also metered parking available on the street, but we do not recommend this as it is very limited and expensive.

For an interactive map with the rates for parking, please visit the following and search for the Nest: https://parking.ubc.ca/map.

Material or Personal Drop-off Details

The closest drop-off area to the Jack Poole Hall would be in by the University Blvd Parking lot or anywhere in the cul-de-sac area closest to Brown's Crafthouse and the Nest. If you require to drop off materials before parking, we suggest dropping them off here and contacting us before hand. Please check your email for our phone details. We will have a team of volunteers near all doors for much of the time between 8:50AM to 10:00AM.

If you require a flatbed to carry large packages, please let us know ahead of time.

Building Details

We are located in the Jack Poole Hall on the 2nd floor of the Robert H. Lee Alumni Centre, which is located in front of the Nest and next to the University Blvd cul-de-sac.

We will have signage and volunteers on the day to direct you to the correct location.

Venue Details

  • The Jack Poole Hall is a large open space for booths.
  • There are no walls or dividers provided between booths in the same row, so please be respectful of your neighbours.
  • We have been told that the estimated amperage is 12A and the estimated voltage is 120V.
  • WiFi is accessible through UBC Public Wifi. See the Internet Access section below for instructions.
  • There are no coat racks so please keep this in mind when packing.

Below is the following floor map for companies. This is subject to change as we work with the catering staff.
Company locations will be marked with table cloth and a paper on the tables with the company name on the day, and a TCF volunteer will help guide your team to your assigned booth.

Catering Menu and Schedule

  • We are excited to offer a variety of food and beverage options for employers throughout the day!
  • Dietary restrictions should be marked on the relevant food items.
  • Employers will be provided with a ticket on the day of the event detailing the number of meals they are entitled to.
  • All lunch items will be placed in the adjoining Bill and Risa Levine Classroom past 12pm. We will send volunteers to give out food in the Jack Poole Hall around 12pm and once more around 1pm.
  • This room is locked unless a TCF volunteer is inside.
  • Should the classroom door be closed, please ask the checkins counter for assistance.
