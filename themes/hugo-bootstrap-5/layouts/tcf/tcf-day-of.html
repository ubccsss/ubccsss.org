Event Day Logistics

< back to main page

This page hosts the logistics for the day of the event for employers. If you have any issues, on the day please contact your nearest TCF Volunteer or message William at the phone number listed in the employer email.

Location Details

We will be hosting the event in the Great Hall of the AMS Nest at UBC.

Parking Details

There are multiple parking lots for employers to park in.

There is also metered parking available on the street, but we do not recommend this as it is very limited and expensive.

For an interactive map with the rates for parking, please visit the followig and search for the Nest: https://parking.ubc.ca/map.

Material or Personal Drop-off Details

The closest drop-off area to the Great Hall would be in by the University Blvd Parking lot or anywhere in the cul-de-sac area closest to Brown's Crafthouse and the Nest.

If you require to drop off materials before parking, we suggest dropping them off here and contacting us before hand.

Please check your email for our phone details.

These details are subject to change depending on the construction activity on campus and will be updated closer to the date.

Shipping Details

If you require shipping to UBC before the day of the event, please notify us via email and provide tracking details at external@ubccsss.org.

Building Details

We are located in the Great Hall on the 2nd floor of the Nest, which is located on the side of the Nest closest to Brown's Clubhouse and the University Blvd cul-de-sac on the second floor.

We will attach a video closer to the date to help you find the location.

We recommend using the elevator by the Great Hall for the fastest movement of materials.

Venue Details

The Great Hall is a large open space with a stage at the front and a large open area for booths.
There are no walls or dividers between booths, so please be respectful of your neighbours.
We are working with building staff to supply power to all booths via extension cords. We are to be given at least 2 outlets per employer.
We have been told that the estimated amperage is 20.
Wifi should be accessible through UBC Public Wifi. See the Internet Access section below for instructions.
There are no coat racks so please keep this in mind when packing.
We will provide a locked and empty room to store any materials. Please contact a volunteer on the day for access as it requires a fob.

Below is the following floor map for companies. This is subject to change as we work with the catering staff.
Company locations will be marked with a paper on the tables with the company name on the day.

Catering Menu

We are excited to offer a variety of food and beverage options for employers and students to enjoy throughout the day!
Dietary restrictions should be marked on the relevant food items as per our catering instructions to our provider.
Employers will be provided with a ticket on the day of the event detailing the number of meals they are entitled to.
Should the purchased food be insufficient in any way, please contact a TCF volunteer and we will give you a gift card to local food vendors.
All food will be placed in the Great Hall foyer. It will be gated off by a TCF volunteer and stanchions from students.

View our menu here.
Please note that the menu is subject to change depending on the catering staff.
