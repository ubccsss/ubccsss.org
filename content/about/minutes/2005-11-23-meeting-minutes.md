<p>3.0 Wine and Cheese</p>
<ul>
<li>ticket sales going slow
</li><li>go door to door selling tickets to faculty
</li><li>take tickets and sell in classrooms
</li><li>space for up to 60 attendees
</li><li>grad students have 25 tickets, sold around 15
</li><li>decorating -&gt; balloons, turn off lights, use candles
</li><li>doors lock at 6pm
</li><li>Jenny is in charge of decorations
</li><li>figure out what we have, then go shopping (Jenny)
</li><li>Chris needs help picking up 50 bottles of wine (Tristan)
</li><li>anyone can serve because it&apos;s a private function
</li></ul>
<p>4.0 Career Fair</p>
<ul>
<li>4 companies registered, 4 more seem definite
</li></ul>
<p>5.0 Day in a Firm</p>
<ul>
<li>visit a company, listen to presentation, tour facilities
</li><li>food usually provided
</li><li>30-40 students attend
</li><li>plan to target EA, IBM, Microsoft, Sierra Systems, Bell, Rogers AT&amp;T, HSBC, Business Objects, Accenture
</li><li>best bet so far are Business Objects and Accenture
</li><li>both were not very interested when approached by MIS because MIS is so small
</li><li>hopefully by partnering with us, this will sweeten the deal and companies will take interest
</li><li>Microsoft probably won&apos;t because they&apos;re in Seattle
</li><li>IT Night: host a discussion, informal networking, and thank yous to companies at UBC
</li><li>contact MIS and find out more information (Mark L.)
</li></ul>
<p>6.0 How to Get a Job Workshop</p>
<ul>
<li>IBM guy was impressed by CS students&apos; knowledge, but not their self-marketing skills
</li><li>discussing the possibility of having a panel discussion sometime in January
</li><li>2 IBM panelists, 3 others
</li><li>focus on breadth -&gt; prepare for IT career beyong just coding
</li><li>have people who actually hire students talk about what they&apos;re looking for
</li><li>bring in HR people, have them look at resumes and talk about what they look for
</li><li>info sessions and career fairs -&gt; usually talk about entry level positions
</li><li>instead, we wish to focus on the big picture, how to move to the next level
</li><li>email Andrew R. with suggestions
</li><li>what&apos;s the path to becoming a manager in the IT field?
</li><li>what are companies looking for in resumes?
</li></ul>
<p>7.0 Getting Information from Committees to Students</p>
<ul>
<li>some committees have useful information -&gt; space committee, teaching and learning
</li><li>we have an undergrad rep on each committee
</li><li>send out emails to reps asking for updates
</li><li>create committee updates section on website
</li></ul>
<p>8.0 Movie Licensing</p>
<ul>
<li>tie in food sales to offset the cost
</li><li>money goes to individual distributer of film
</li></ul>
<p>9.0 CUTC Funding</p>
<ul>
